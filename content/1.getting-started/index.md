ZisK runs on Linux and macOS. Linux is the recommended platform; it supports all proving backends, GPU acceleration, and distributed proving. macOS works well for development and testing.

Pick your platform, install ZisK, and then follow the Quickstart to create your first project and generate a proof.

- [Install on Linux](/getting-started/install-linux): full platform with native backends
- [Install on macOS](/getting-started/install-macos): development setup with emulator backend
- [Quickstart](/getting-started/getting-started-with-zisk): your first project and proof
